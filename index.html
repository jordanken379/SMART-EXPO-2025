ABOUT

SMART EXPO 2025 is an exciting annual event organized by OSIS of SMAN 3 Tondano, bringing together students, teachers, and alumni for a day of exploration and celebration. This offline event will be held on February 5, 2025, at SMAN 3 Tondano.

The expo serves as a platform for extracurricular clubs to showcase their activities, talents, and achievements. Visitors will have the opportunity to discover various student organizations, watch live performances, and participate in interactive sessions.

In addition to club exhibitions, SMART EXPO 2025 also features a bazaar where attendees can enjoy a variety of delicious food and merchandise sold by students. It's a perfect chance to experience the vibrant school community while supporting student initiatives.
